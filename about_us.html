About Us

There are three things that characterize our team. First up, we believe in sustainability. Secondly, we want more people to care about our natural world. And thirdly, we see our humble Bloom pencils as simple and fun ways to make this happen.

By planting a Bloom pencil instead of throwing it out you can make sustainability visible to others. And inspire them to make changes in their daily life. This is the idea behind our project.
