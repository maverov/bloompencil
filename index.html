Small everyday items with big messages

You're going to fall in love with nature

Bloom pencils are the first-ever plantable pencil. They're made of sustainable wood and look like a classic pencil, except in place of an eraser tip, each pencil has a small biodegradable capsule full of vegetable, herb or flower seeds.

When you're done using the pencil, you simply poke the eraser end of the pencil into a small pot of soil to get started. Simply "add sunlight & water" and after tending to the seeds for a moment, you should be rewarded with your own lovely little plant.
